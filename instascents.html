Challenge

Instascents is an e-commerce fragrance retailer with a diverse product catalog. The site was suffering from technical SEO issues, poor product page optimization, and low conversion rates despite having decent traffic.

  • Technical SEO issues affecting crawlability and indexation
  • Weak product page optimization and duplicate content
  • Poor internal linking structure for product discovery
  • Slow page load times impacting user experience

Solution

Technical Audit & Fixes

Conducted comprehensive technical audit identifying and fixing crawl issues, XML sitemap problems, and indexation issues.

Product Page Optimization

Optimized all product pages with unique descriptions, proper schema markup, and keyword targeting.

Internal Linking Strategy

Implemented strategic internal linking to improve product discovery and distribute page authority.

Performance Optimization

Optimized images, implemented lazy loading, and reduced page load times for better user experience.
