Challenge

The Psychology Hub is an educational platform providing psychology resources and mental health information. The website had valuable content but was struggling with organic visibility and search engine rankings.

  • Low organic traffic compared to competitors
  • Poor keyword targeting and ranking positions
  • Outdated content structure and optimization
  • Missing metadata and on-page SEO elements

Solution

Comprehensive Keyword Research

Conducted in-depth keyword analysis to identify high-value psychology-related search terms with strong search intent.

Content Optimization

Optimized existing content and created new targeted articles focusing on high-potential keywords.

Technical SEO

Improved site structure, meta tags, headers, internal linking, and schema markup implementation.

Link Building Strategy

Developed and executed ethical link building campaigns to increase domain authority.
